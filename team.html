Meet Our Team

Get to know the talented individuals behind Echoeback.

JONATHAN SCHWARTZ

Founder

Over the past three years, my journey has been one of immense personal growth, though not without its challenges. I often found myself repeating mistakes, relearning lessons I thought I had mastered. My passion for helping others was always there, but I knew I didn’t want to be a personal coach.

One day, I saw a video of a woman who transformed her life in five months by recording heartfelt messages to her future self. Her journey was incredibly inspiring and sparked an idea within me. This powerful method of self-reflection and motivation should be accessible to everyone.

From this inspiration, Echoeback was born. I am proud to offer a platform for people to document their thoughts, emotions, and goals, allowing them to look back on their progress with pride. Echoeback helps you capture your journey and inspire your future.
